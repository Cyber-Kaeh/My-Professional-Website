Rules

Click on either of the dice to roll.
If both dice equal 7 or 11 you win instantly.
If you roll 2, 3, or 12 on the first roll you lose.
If you roll 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10 on your first roll that becomes your "point." You continue to roll aiming to match your point to win. If you roll a 7 instead of your point you lose.
